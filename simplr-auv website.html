Autonomous Maritime ISR

SIMPLR–AUV

A low-cost autonomous underwater vehicle for intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance in shallow littoral and inland-freshwater waters.

In development · software operational, vehicle build-ready
Scroll

A complete, software-first approach to maritime ISR — pairing an affordable autonomous platform with a full operations and analysis suite — designed to reach the shallow, confined, and hard-to-access waters where larger systems can’t easily operate.

The platform
Built around three ideas

Low cost, high capability

Capability comparable to conventional systems at a small fraction of their cost — achieved through smart software and commodity hardware rather than expensive sensors.

Software-first

A complete autonomy, sensing, and operations stack — developed and validated before the hardware — so missions are configurations, not fixed platform identity.

Modular by design

A flexible substrate with swappable mission payloads and a layered compute architecture, intended to adapt across missions and environments.
